Welcome to Sitting Pretty

Located in the coastal suburb of Mindarie in the Northern Suburbs of Perth, WA, Photographer Nicole Weston specialises in Portrait and Nature inspired Photography Her Style is unique, fresh, honest, simple and natural.

Nicole’s specialty is personalised portraits celebrating people, relationships, family and individuals with an emphasis on children’s portraits, capturing natural expressions and character.

She also has a passion for nature and the amazing art that exists within it. Nicole’s range of Nature and Landscape Art images are available as home décor, greeting cards and other gift ideas.

This is my unique view of the world. I want to share it with you.
